Club History

Great Linford Football Club was formed in 1993/1994 season. It has always been developed as a club to serve the local community. The club's teams span junior and youth age groups from 5 up to 18 years old. We also have two senior teams for adults of 16 and over! The club currently has 8 teams in 5 leagues. All of our coaches and managers are F.A. qualified or are scheduled to attend FA Level 1 minimum courses Each season, the commitment is to make sure that it is essentially a family club where footballers of all abilities can develop.
